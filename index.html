Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing strategy where businesses reward affiliates for each customer brought by their marketing efforts.

  • Low-risk way to expand marketing reach
  • Pay only for actual results
  • Leverage other people's audiences
  • Scale without upfront advertising costs

Key benefits for startups:

  • Cost-effective customer acquisition
  • Access to established audiences
  • Performance-based payment model
  • Rapid market expansion potential
  • Built-in social proof from affiliates

Typical conversion rates: 2-5% for digital products, 1-3% for physical products.

Steps to launch:

  1. Set up tracking system
  2. Create affiliate resources
  3. Recruit quality partners
  4. Monitor and optimize

Popular platforms: ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, Impact Radius

Conversion Optimization

Conversion optimization is the practice of increasing the percentage of visitors who complete desired actions on your website.

Average improvements: 20-30% conversion rate increase through systematic testing and optimization.

Focus areas: landing pages, checkout process, call-to-action buttons, form optimization.

High-impact tactics:

  • A/B testing headlines and CTAs
  • Reducing form friction
  • Adding social proof elements
  • Improving page load speed
  • Mobile optimization
  • Clear value propositions

Essential tools:

  • Google Analytics & Google Optimize
  • Hotjar for user behavior analysis
  • Optimizely for advanced testing
  • Unbounce for landing pages
  • Crazy Egg for heatmap analysis
